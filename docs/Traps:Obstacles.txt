---OBSTACLE TYPES----

This doc includes the initial design of trap types and obstacles to be included in the final version of Moonfern.

The design aims to incorporate those structures to the environment of the game. They should feel natural within the imagined world of Moonfern.

----------------

1. Swamp

Enemies that step into it start drowning, and eventually die after being fully submerged.

2. Deadly vines

3 seconds after Nyra runs underneath, the vines fall on the enemies, wrapping around their bodies and eventually choking them to death.

3. Vine jump

Nyra can grab hanging vines and swing across large gaps, launching herself forward with momentum to reach distant platforms or hidden areas. Enemies are left behind.

4. Poisonous flower

Immediately after Nyra runs through, the flowers release toxic gas. Enemies are poisoned and die.
